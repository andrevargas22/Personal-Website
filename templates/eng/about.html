About me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer based in São Paulo, Brazil, with experience in developing and deploying ML and MLOps solutions with a solid background in Computer Science and Software Engineering, cloud architectures, CI/CD, and implementing models and APIs in production.

My journey began with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering from the Universidade de Brasília, which provided a strong foundation in electronics, microcontrollers, hardware design, and embedded firmware programming. An exchange program at Cardiff University further broadened my technical and cultural horizons.

Initially, I worked as a Hardware Engineer, focusing on IoT, industrial automation and digital transformation. This period allowed me to hone my skills in practical, hands-on environments, bridging the gap between physical systems and digital data. My work in hardware and electronics laid a critical groundwork for understanding complex systems.

A few years into my career, my fascination with data and its potential to drive innovation led me to pivot towards Data Science and Machine Learning. I embraced this new direction by diving deep into machine learning models, cloud-based architectures, and MLOps practices. Today, I leverage my diverse background to develop and implement scalable machine learning solutions, driving efficiency and innovation in various projects.

I am passionate about studying and am largely self-taught. Despite not being a web designer by trade, I designed and built this website myself, incorporating cool features using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, all of which I learned on my own.

Outside of my professional life, I'm fortunate to share my life with my beautiful wife, Maria, whose support and love inspire me every day. I enjoy spending time with her and my dog, Bibi, who adds joy and companionship to our routine. I'm also an avid gamer and a music enthusiast, often found behind a drum set. These personal passions enrich my life, providing balance and perspective that I bring into my work.

I seek opportunities to expand MLOps skills, contributing to Machine Learning projects' advancement, efficiency, and scalability.
